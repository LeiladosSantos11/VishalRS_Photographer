About:

My name is Vishal RS, born in a little budding town of Uttar Pradesh (Aligarh, India) and brought up in Bangalore. My work is focused mostly on photography, videography, writing & traveling.

As a film and digital photographer, I specialize in capturing reality amidst the chaos. Pièce de résistance of my work is photography & filmmaking for brand promotion in lifestyle, travel & journalism genres.

I was awarded a merit for my recent Degree in Photography from London College of Communication, UAL. My prior experience is a projectile between fashion, communication & design which now manifests an overarching outlook.

I now hunt for my next extemporaneous assignment that challenges my frontal lobe, ignites alternate dimensional conversations and brews incredible coffee.

My perspective widened as my brain started to develop new neurons. Besides the urge to experience, challenge & impact, I wish to explore.

The simple idea behind being a part of something more than just my space, teaches me more than I could do without.
